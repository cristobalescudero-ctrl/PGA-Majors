How to Enter

  • Pick 5 players from the field.
  • Pick a 6th alternate — replaces a pick that withdraws before teeing off.
  • Add a tiebreaker: predict the winner's score to par.
  • Submit to jrodriguez@holtlogistics.com and Jeffreyarnold909@gmail.com by the deadline.
  • Enter as many teams as you like at $25 each.

Scoring

  • Need ≥3 picks to make the cut to qualify.
  • The best 3 scores (lowest to par) of your qualifying picks count.
  • Entry with the lowest combined score wins.
  • Ties broken by closest tiebreaker. Still tied = split the prize.

Prize Pool — $25 per entry

  • 70% → 1st Place
  • 20% → 2nd Place
  • 10% → 3rd Place
